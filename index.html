Core Differentiator

Advanced delivery systems

For brands that want to lead, not follow. These technologies deliver real functional advantages.

Sustained Release Pellets

Extended ingredient release over 8–12 hours. Superior bioavailability and consumer compliance.

Dual Chamber Capsules

Separate incompatible actives within a single dose. Protects stability until point of absorption.

Liquid + Solid Systems

Oil-soluble and water-soluble ingredients in one delivery. Enhanced bioavailability for lipid-based actives.

Multi-Phase Delivery

Staged dissolution profiles for complex multi-ingredient formulations. Precision timing, maximized synergy.
